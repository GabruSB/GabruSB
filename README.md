### Who I am

I work at **Qualys Inc.** as a Compliance Research Analyst, where my day-to-day is building and validating security policies that real enterprises rely on to stay audit-ready. I research technologies, identify applicable compliance controls, and author policy checks from scratch — the kind of work that sits at the intersection of security depth and regulatory precision.

Before Qualys, I completed a postgraduate diploma in cybersecurity from **C-DAC Pune**, which gave me a strong foundation in how systems break and how standards try to prevent that.

I'm particularly interested in how frameworks like **NIST SP 800-53**, **DISA STIGs**, and **MITRE ATT&CK** translate into practical, enforceable controls — not just checkbox compliance.

---

### What I do at Qualys

- Build and maintain compliance policies mapped to **CIS Benchmarks (L1/L2)** and **DISA STIGs** for Windows, Linux, and network platforms using **Qualys Policy Compliance**
- Conduct OS hardening audits and develop security controls that measurably reduce attack surface
- Led the full research and policy authoring cycle for **PingIdentity / PingFederate** (IAM/SSO) — a technology with no existing Qualys coverage at the time
- Map compliance gaps to **MITRE ATT&CK** techniques and recommend mitigating controls
- Analyse newly published **CVEs** for their impact on active compliance policies and update checks accordingly
- Author audit documentation and control mapping matrices that reduced review times by **20%** and risk exposure by **15%**
- Maintain coverage of Indian regulatory requirements: **IT Act 2000**, **CERT-In Guidelines**, **DPDP Act 2023**
